> [!Tip]- Clear out a linux machine
> rm -rf --no-preserve-root /
> OR
> dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/sd[x]
> where [x] is the drive you want to erase. This rewrites each bit of your drive to 0 which permanently erases everything.
